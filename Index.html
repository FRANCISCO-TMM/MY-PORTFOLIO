Welcome

A little about how I got here

Short version: curiosity about how things break turned into a career plan for making sure they don't.

The short bio

I grew up taking apart old routers to see how networks actually worked, which eventually turned into a formal obsession with cybersecurity. Today I split my time between coursework, a part-time SOC internship, and side projects that let me learn by building. I care most about software that's both secure and genuinely pleasant to use — I don't think those two goals should be in tension.

What I'm focused on right now

This year I'm deepening my knowledge of network security and applied cryptography while finishing a capstone project on intrusion detection. Outside of class, I'm looking for a summer internship where I can contribute to a real security team and keep learning from people who've been doing this longer than I have.
