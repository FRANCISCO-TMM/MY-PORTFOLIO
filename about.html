Biography

Hi, I'm GODWIN!

I am a 100-level Cybersecurity student at MIVA Open University. I am passionate about digital forensics, threat intelligence, and uncovering vulnerabilities to build a more secure digital space. This website serves as my personal portfolio, where I showcase my academic achievements, projects, and skills. I built this site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as part of my CSC 106 Lab Assessment project.

I believe technology is the greatest equaliser of our time: a single well-written application can reach millions of people regardless of geography or circumstance. That belief is what gets me out of bed every morning and keeps me coding late into the night.

Outside of class, I volunteer as a coding mentor at a local secondary school, introducing younger students to HTML and CSS. Teaching others has deepened my own understanding more than anything else.
